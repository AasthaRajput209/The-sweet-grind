Our Story.

Started in a garage.
Built on Texas obsession.

The Sweet Grind began in 2022 with a single espresso machine, a hand-painted truck, and a stubborn belief that a coffee shop on wheels could be every bit as crafted as the best brick-and-mortar cafés.

Today we pull thousands of shots a week across Austin — but the standard hasn't budged: small-batch syrups, single-origin beans, ceremonial-grade matcha, and desserts pulled from the oven the morning they're served.
